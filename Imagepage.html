Must See Places in Nigeria

All images and events where Documented by us

Rayfield Resort Jos

Rayfield located in Jos has an amazing natural water body which has boats and a few jetski which you could rent for an hour and more , we got to go on the boat rides for very afforable prices to be honest the cheapest we have seen in the country.

Shehu Musa Yar'adua Centre

The exhibition of the late shehu Musa Yar'adua's life regalia is one to behold the have so much to see here from his diary to his clothings and even valuable pieces from the Nigerian Civil War

BTM GARDEN

Such a lovely cozy place, the ambience and the environment is a mixture of Africa heritage with a modern touch very nature inspired, good for photo shoot and romance The area is vast, we walked around and crossed a tiny bridge to reach the mini zoo, then there are food stalls and souvenir shops. Only limited animals, birds in the cage to see, but the place itself is relaxing. The zoo has a minimal fee. The parking is huge, really good for family and big group outings.

Crest Hotels Jos

My stay at the hotel was pleasant that I felt so comfortable extending my stay. The staff at the hotel were friendly and accommodating. Generally, the environment of the hotel was properly cleaned and it was conducive for relaxation. It has and Old days feeling to it that just gives a warmth, it made me feel like i was in a castle as a chef or someone of royal blood.
